Write the elements of the tree below in the order they would be seen by a pre-order, in-order, and post-order traversal.

      ______19
     /        \
   47___       63
  /     \        \
23       -2       94
        /           \
      55             28
	  
	  
	  
	  
	  
Solution:

pre-order	
19 47 23 -2 55 63 94 28
in-order	
23, 47, 55, -2, 19, 63, 94, 28 
post-order	
23, 55, -2, 47, 28, 94, 63, 19
